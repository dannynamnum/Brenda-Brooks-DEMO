The Canyon Lifestyle

Built for the whole family

The Canyons were designed by GL Homes as all-ages communities — resort amenities, A-rated schools, and a guard gate that lets kids ride bikes until dusk.

The Clubhouse

An 8,000 sq ft resort, steps from your door

Every Canyon community centers on a clubhouse with a resort-style pool, a separate wading and splash area for the little ones, a fitness center, a social hall with catering kitchen, and a kids' playroom — the kind of amenities families actually use every weekend.

Active Living

Courts, fields and friends nearby

Har-Tru clay tennis courts, basketball, volleyball, an indoor sport court and ping-pong — the Canyons are made for active families. It's a community where weekends fill themselves and neighbors become friends.

Location & Schools

A-rated schools, minutes to everything

Served by A-rated Palm Beach County schools and moments from Canyon Town Center, with the Boynton Beach Boulevard Turnpike exit just outside the gate. Beaches, dining and shopping are a short drive — the Canyons sit right where families want to be.
